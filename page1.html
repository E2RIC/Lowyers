Types of Services Lawyers Can Provide

Attorneys perform different functions which vary in cost and the level of control clients have over their affairs, including:

Consultation for legal information
advice Consultation to review documents (such as agreements, leases, wills, corporation documents, do-it-yourself court forms) Mediation, arbitration,
or other third party services Preparation of documents
Representing clients in negotiations (possibly under “collaborative lawyering” agreement where both attorneys withdraw if either side chooses to litigate) Representing clients in court, arbitration, administrative and legislative hearings
